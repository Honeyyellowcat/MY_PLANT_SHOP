CARE AND HEALTH FOR
YOUR PLANTS

Care Guide:

  • For your Monstera and Swiss Cheese Plant, water once a week during the growing season (spring and summer), allowing the top inch of soil to dry out between waterings. In colder months, reduce watering frequency to once every two weeks.

  • Your Pink Syngonium prefers consistently moist soil, so water it when the top inch of soil feels dry. Aim to water it more frequently than the Monstera and Pothos, typically every 5-7 days during the growing season.

  • Pothos, being a more resilient plant, can tolerate drying out between waterings. Water it thoroughly once every 1-2 weeks, allowing the soil to dry out partially between waterings.

  • Ensure all plants have good drainage to prevent waterlogging, but be especially cautious with your Swiss Cheese Plant and Monstera. These plants can suffer if water accumulates around their roots.

  • Place your Monstera, Swiss Cheese Plant, Pink Syngonium, and Pothos in locations with bright, indirect sunlight. Avoid exposing them to direct sunlight, especially during the hottest parts of the day, to prevent leaf scorching.
